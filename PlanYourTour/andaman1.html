Overview

If you are looking for a peaceful and relaxing vacation, there is no better option than the Andaman and Nicobar tours. With crystal clear water, these islands have so much nature to offer with their history and diverse marine life.

Key information about the package:

Starting point: Port Blair
Endpoint: Port Blair
Duration of trip: 5 Days 4 Nights
Route details: Port Blair - Havelock Island - Kalapathar beach - Port Blair

Tour Package Inclusions:

  • Accommodation in a hotel or camp as per the selection
  • Buffet breakfast on all days
  • Buffet dinner on all days
  • Sightseeing as mentioned in the itinerary
  • Experienced travel guide
  • Driver and transportation
  • All types of applicable taxes

Itinerary

Day1 : Arrival at Port Blair and a trip to cellular jail

  • Arrive in Port Blair for pick-up and hotel check-in
  • Experience the beauty of Corbyn Cove Beach
  • Beach visits and sports activities
  • Trip to Cellular Jail and enjoy the light and sound show
  • Overnight stay at hotel

Day2 : Visit Havelock Island and Radhanagar Beach

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Travel to Havelock Island
  • Enjoy activities at Radhanagar Beach
  • Overnight stay at a hotel on Havelock Island

Day 3 : Explore Havelock

  • Breakfast at a hotel
  • Explore Havelock Island
  • Spend time on the beach
  • Rest till the evening
  • Overnight stay at a hotel on Havelock Island

Day4 : Visit kalapathar Beach

  • Breakfast at a hotel in Havelock
  • Check out from the hotel
  • Visit Kalapathar beach
  • Travel to Port Blair
  • Overnight stay in the hotel

Day5: Rohtang - Travel to Rohtang Pass Sightseeing of lahaul valley and Chandra River

  • Breakfast at a hotel in Manali
  • Travel to Rohtang Pass
  • Sightseeing of Lahaul valley and Chandra river
  • Some quality time at Rohtang Pass and trekking
  • Travel back to the hotel in Manali
  • Dinner in hotel
  • Overnight stay in the hotel

Day 6: Bye bye Port Blair

  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel
  • Check out from the hotel
